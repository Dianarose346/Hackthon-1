About Me

Hi, I'm Dianarose, a 24-year-old who loves technology and building websites. My journey into tech started when I was 14, but life took me in a different direction. I studied Finance, graduated, and for a while, working in tech felt like a far off dream.

Then, I got a chance to volunteer in the tech industry, met amazing people, and realized I truly belong in this world. I didn't know how to start, but the PLP Scholarship Program gave me the guidance I needed. Now, I'm learning to become a full-stack web developer, creating websites that are both useful and easy to use.

I work with HTML, CSS, and Python. I believe anyone can learn tech if they practice and build real projects. I'm driven by curiosity, creativity, and the excitement of solving problems with code.
